About Me

Hello, and welcome to my bio page. Concerning my working background, I have over twenty years of experience in the fields of Software Training and Instructional Design. I have worked in the sectors of Healthcare, Department of Defense, and Education.

I lived in Germany for about five years, and while I lived there I learned to speak German, traveled Europe, and worked for the U.S. Army. While working for the U.S. Army Research Medical Unit as a Research Assistant, I assisted in compiling data that measured soldier morale.

For hobbies, I like to play the guitar, and I also practice martial arts, yoga and meditation.
